Overview Comparison

This page summarizes the methods on four axes: model dependence, constraint handling, robustness, and computation. Larger values mean more of that property.

1 = low 3 = medium 5 = high

Model dependence

How much plant structure or a usable model is needed before the method is practical.

Constraints

Whether the method handles actuator and state limits directly instead of as an afterthought.

Robustness

How deliberately the method accounts for model mismatch, disturbances, and uncertainty.

Computation

How expensive the online implementation is relative to the classical baseline.
