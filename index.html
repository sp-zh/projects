Engineering workflow

A Practical Escalation Path

1

Measure the plant

Identify bandwidth, delay, saturation, noise, disturbance sources, and failure limits before selecting a controller.

2

Start with the simplest loop

Use PID or feedforward PID when the plant is mostly SISO and the required guarantees are modest.

3

Move to state space

Use pole placement, LQR, LQI, and Kalman filtering when states, coupling, and model structure matter.

4

Escalate for constraints or uncertainty

Use MPC for hard limits, H∞ or μ-synthesis for uncertainty certificates, and RL only with a safe training pipeline.
